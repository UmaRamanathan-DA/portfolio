Strategic Consulting Project - Digital Retail Bank Launch Strategy for Client X

Organization: Synpulse Consulting

Location: United Kingdom

Project Type: Academic (Strathclyde Business School)

Duration: Mar 2024 – Apr 2024

Role: Strategy Consultant

Project Description

Supported vendor evaluation and go-to-market strategy for Client X's UK digital bank launch, focusing on BaaS selection, regulatory alignment, and digital-first product design.

Performed a comprehensive multi-criteria decision analysis (MCDA) to evaluate 8 Banking-as-a-Service (BaaS) vendors across 13 critical factors; recommended Thought Machine for its modular architecture and long-term strategic alignment. Designed a detailed go-to-market plan, including customer segmentation, product roadmap, and digital channel strategy tailored to UK regulatory frameworks (FCA, PRA, Open Banking). Proposed a cloud-native, API-first architecture to maximize agility, scalability, and compliance. Recommended digital capabilities such as AI-driven personal finance tools, Buy Now Pay Later (BNPL), embedded finance, and ESG-conscious offerings targeting urban professionals aged 25–55.

Detailed Tasks

  • Conducted market research and competitor analysis to identify opportunities and threats in the UK digital banking sector.
  • Developed a phased go-to-market strategy, including vendor evaluation and BaaS selection.
